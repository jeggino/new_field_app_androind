How to use the app

  • Create new project creates an empty GeoJSON FeatureCollection.
  • Open existing project loads a GeoJSON file from your device.
  • Use the + button to start adding an observation.
  • A cross appears in the center of the map. Move the map until the cross is exactly where the observation belongs.
  • Select bat or bird, choose the species, date, time, and comments.
  • Click any observation marker to edit or delete it.
  • The app asks for confirmation before editing or deleting an observation.
  • The map can show normal OpenStreetMap tiles or satellite imagery using the map layer switcher.
  • Existing GeoJSON lines, polygons, and multi-geometries are also drawn on the map.
